About This Portal

A standalone, offline-capable collaboration portal. No server or database required — all uploaded data stays in your local browser session.

How to Use

Step 1Open Module 1 to track UAT test case progress by uploading an Excel or CSV file.
Step 2Columns needed: Test Case ID, Name, Tester, Status, Start Date, End Date.
Step 3Open Module 2 to monitor issues and SLA compliance with your issue tracker export.
Step 4Use filters, date ranges, and status chips to slice data. Export reports as needed.
Step 5Toggle role badge (Test Admin / End User) to reflect your current context.
